Personal Site Plan
Site Name - Game Optimization Hub
Site Purpose
    The purpose of this website is to help PC gamers improve their game performance by providing clear optimization guides based on their graphics card. The site will explain which graphics settings affect performance the most and how users can adjust them to increase FPS without sacrificing too much visual quality.
Target Audience
    The target audience is PC gamers who want better performance in their games. Many players do not fully understand what each graphics setting does or which settings impact performance the most. This site will help beginner and intermediate gamers quickly find recommended settings for their GPU.
Elevator Pitch
    Game Optimization Hub helps gamers get the best performance out of their PC. By selecting their graphics card, users can view recommended settings, optimization guides, and tips to increase FPS and reduce lag in popular games.
